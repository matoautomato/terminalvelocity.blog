Alternating chapters follow the cultural evolution of Portia labiata spiders over generations and the desperate human crew of a generation ship running out of options. A nanovirus accelerates spider intelligence through millennia of civilisational development. First contact between species who are both "children" of Old Earth.



## Review

If you are an arachnophobe, you should not read this book under any circumstance. I mean it. If that does not bother you, it is definitely a fascinating thought experiment of evolutionary pathways. For me the end - first contact of 2 different species - was a bit weak.
