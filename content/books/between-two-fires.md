France, 1348. A fallen knight and a peasant girl who sees angels must reach Avignon to deliver a message to the Pope. The plague is no natural disaster – it's cover for a war in heaven, and the demons stalking them are terrifyingly real. Dark medieval horror that reads like Cormac McCarthy writing Dante's Inferno.



## Review

I don't have many words to waste: this book kept me *glued* to my e-reader and was one of the best medieval horror fantasy books I have ever read. If you haven't read it, you *should* have FOMO.
